About Us

Experience You Can Trust. Results You Can Measure.

RedWave Consulting, LLC is a broadband engineering and fiber network consulting firm serving carriers, MSOs, municipalities, and contractors across the region. We bring field-proven expertise to every phase of your network — from initial design and permitting through construction support, fiber splicing, and final testing.

Founded and led by Patrick Houston, RedWave is built on a foundation of real-world telecom experience spanning 15+ years across design, field operations, project management, and executive leadership. Every engagement is backed by a team that has done this work firsthand — at scale, on deadline, and to spec.

Whether you're deploying new fiber infrastructure, expanding HFC plant, or need precise OTDR testing and light path verification, RedWave is your trusted partner in the field and at the design table.
