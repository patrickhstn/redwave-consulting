About Us

Experience You Can Trust. Results You Can Measure.

RedWave Consulting, LLC is a broadband engineering and fiber network consulting firm serving carriers, MSOs, municipalities, and contractors across the region. We bring field-proven expertise to every phase of your network — from initial design and permitting through construction support, fiber splicing, and final testing.

Founded and led by Patrick Houston, RedWave is built on a foundation of real-world telecom experience spanning 15+ years across design, field operations, project management, and executive leadership. Every engagement is backed by a team that has done this work firsthand — at scale, on deadline, and to spec.

Whether you're deploying new fiber infrastructure, expanding HFC plant, or need precise OTDR testing and light path verification, RedWave is your trusted partner in the field and at the design table.

What We Do

Core Service Offerings

Comprehensive outside plant field engineering including site walks, make-ready assessments, pole surveys, strand mapping, and construction support for aerial and underground fiber and HFC deployments.
Precision fusion and mechanical splicing for single-mode and multimode fiber optic cables. We ensure low-loss, high-quality splices on aerial, underground, and inside plant applications — with full documentation provided.
Detailed optical time-domain reflectometer (OTDR) testing to characterize fiber spans, identify faults, and verify splice/connector loss. Full light path verification ensures every circuit meets spec before turn-up.
End-to-end design of fiber and HFC networks optimized for construction, serviceability, and long-term plant maintenance. Experienced with greenfield builds, upgrades, and last-mile access network design.
Geospatial data creation, fiber mapping, and network documentation using industry platforms including ESRI, AutoCAD, and MicroStation. We produce accurate, construction-ready plant records and presentation-quality maps.
On-site and remote project management for broadband construction programs. We coordinate field crews, track production schedules, manage quality control, and serve as the point of accountability between client and contractor.
ETL translations and data migration between GIS, CAD, and database platforms. We convert legacy plant records into modern engineering systems, ensuring accuracy and continuity of your network documentation.
On-demand consulting for carriers, utilities, and municipalities. From design platform evaluation and staff augmentation to custom reporting and data analysis, RedWave delivers the technical horsepower your team needs.
